Policy Statement
Purpose

This Policy Statement and its associated Policies are reviewed annually by the XPS IT Department to ensure the Company’s information systems and information assets are secure.

The Company is increasingly dependent upon its information systems, and the information they contain, to enable safe and effective business operations and the delivery of business objectives. The value of the Company’s information to itself and to other organisations is significant. Therefore, the Company must implement effective security measures to protect its information systems and information assets.

The purpose of this Policy is to ensure:

  1. accountability and responsibility of information security is established;
  2. information assets are protected against unauthorised access or use;
  3. confidentiality of information is maintained;
  4. integrity of information is preserved through protection from unauthorised modification;
  5. availability of information to authorised users when needed is preserved;
  6. regular review and assessment to enable continuous improvement of information security measures; and
  7. regulatory and legislative requirements are met.
Scope

This document should be reviewed by those directly involved with XPS data processing operations. Specifically:

  1. Day-to-day data processing operations (including IT systems).
  2. Implementation of new data processing systems.
  3. Maintenance of existing data processing.

This document should also be used for reference purposes when XPS undertakes its annual compliance review.
